Objectives

Goal

Longitudinal trend of blood based biomarkers to monitor and identify Alzheimers disease.

Current standard

CSF (Cerebro Spinal Fluid) bio Markers are the current standard.

Alzheimers Identification

Amyloid beta peptides are most commonly used to identify Alzheimers disease. More recently Tau Phosphorylation epitope 217 has been identified as a potentially useful blood based bio marker.

Variables of Interest

  • Plasma P-tau-217 - Outcome Variable
  • Time - Key Predictor
  • Amyloid Beta Positvity - Key Categorical Predictor
  • Age - Covariate
  • Sex - Covariate

Longitudnal Design

  • Longitudnal growth model with random slopes and Intercepts.
  • Limited Model information provided.
  • No Random Effects Interpreted.
  • Age & Sex Covariates were largely ignored.
  • No measures of uncertainty were reported.
